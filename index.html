Frequently Asked Questions

If you're getting good reach but not generating leads, you might need to refine your targeting and call-to-action. Ensure your ads and content are engaging and direct users to a clear, compelling landing page.

If you’re getting leads but not converting them, consider improving your follow-up process. Use personalized communication, provide valuable information, and address any concerns promptly to increase conversion rates.

If users add items to their cart but don’t complete the purchase, there could be issues with the checkout process or shipping costs. Simplify the checkout experience and ensure there are no unexpected costs.

To gain followers, focus on creating engaging and valuable content that resonates with your target audience. Use relevant hashtags, interact with users, and run promotions or contests to boost visibility.
